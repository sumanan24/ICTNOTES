System Analysis and Design

Module Code: K72T001M02 / Level 05

Compulsory Relevant Unit: K72T001U02 100 Notional Hours 60 Theory 30 Practical 10 Self-study

Learning Outcomes

General Objective

At the end of this module, students will understand system analysis and design concepts, acquire skills to apply system analysis techniques, and know system requirements specification document preparation.

Specific Objectives

At the end of this module the student shall be able to:

  1. Analyze requirements specified in documentation
  2. Apply system analysis techniques for logical design
  3. Identify components and document functionality and data
  4. Document requirement outcomes according to standard templates
  5. Develop test cases based on design specifications
  6. Prepare the final requirement document

Learning Content / Topics

1. Introduction to Systems Analysis (20 hrs.)
2. Introduction to Systems Design (20 hrs.)
3. System Requirements Specification Document (20 hrs.)

2 hrs Application Apply SOP to conduct reviews, accommodate feedbacks, document outcomes, and obtain approval The teacher guides application and analysis of review and approval processes Students apply SOP through role-play to review documents, accommodate feedbacks, document outcomes, and obtain approval Role-play scenarios, Design documents, Distribution templates, SOP documents
10 min Consolidation Evaluate review and approval processes and synthesize key concepts The teacher evaluates student work and guides synthesis Students evaluate their processes and synthesize learning Student work, Summary
