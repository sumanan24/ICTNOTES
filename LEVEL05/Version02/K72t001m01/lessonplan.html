User Requirements for Software Solution

Module Code: K72T001M01 / Level 05

Compulsory Relevant Unit: K72T001U01 100 Notional Hours 60 Theory 30 Practical 10 Self-study

Learning Outcomes

General Objective

At the end of this module, students will understand business processes, acquire knowledge of requirement gathering, and know requirement analysis and feasibility study report preparation.

Specific Objectives

At the end of this module the student shall be able to:

  1. Analyze business processes and identify key stakeholders
  2. Apply requirements gathering techniques and analyze strengths and weaknesses
  3. Break down requirements and define project scope
  4. Evaluate feasibility aspects of software solutions
  5. Prepare and present reports

Practical Assignments

Assignment 01: Prepare a user requirement document

Students will gather requirements for a selected business organization and prepare a comprehensive user requirement document.

Assignment 02: Prepare a feasibility study report for a given industry specific scenario

Students will conduct a feasibility study covering technical, economical, practical, organizational, and legal aspects for a given scenario.

Assessment & Weighting

Continuous Assessment:
Assessment Description Weightage
Individual Assignment Prepare a user requirement document and feasibility study report for a given industry specific scenario 40%
Written Assessment 01 Topics: Introduction to various business processes, Study of business processes, Requirements Gathering 10%
Written Assessment 02 Topics: Requirement analysis, Feasibility study 10%
Summative Assessment:
Assessment Description Weightage
