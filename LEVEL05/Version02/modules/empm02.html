Workplace Communications Management

Module Code: EMPM02

50 Notional Hours 30 Lectures/Tutorials 15 Practical/Industry Visits 5 Self-study

Module Overview

This module develops professional communication skills essential for effective workplace interactions and management.

Learning Objectives

General Objective:

To develop comprehensive understanding and practical skills in workplace communications management.

Specific Objectives:
  • Understand fundamental concepts and principles
  • Apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios
  • Develop professional competencies
  • Analyze and solve real-world problems
  • Demonstrate proficiency through projects and assessments

Lesson Plan

Detailed lesson plans are structured across 30 hours of lectures/tutorials, 15 hours of practical work and industry visits, and 5 hours of self-study. The module includes theoretical sessions, hands-on practical exercises, and comprehensive assessments.

Teaching Methods:
  • Interactive lectures and presentations
  • Hands-on practical sessions
  • Industry visits and case studies
  • Group discussions and collaborative projects
  • Self-directed learning activities

Assessment Plan

Assessment Type Description Weightage
Continuous Assessment Regular assignments and quizzes 30%
Practical Project Hands-on project work 40%
Written Examination Theoretical knowledge test 20%
Portfolio Collection of work samples 10%
