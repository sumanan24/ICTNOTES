What is an SLA?

üìã

SLA = Service Level Agreement

In simple words: It's a promise about how fast the help desk will help you.

Think of it like: When you order pizza, they promise "delivery in 30 minutes or less." That's their promise to you. An SLA is the same - a promise about when help will arrive!

Why is SLA Important?

1
You Know What to Expect
No more guessing when help will come
2
They Must Keep Their Promise
The help desk has to meet their deadlines
3
Better Service for You
You get faster, better help
4
Everyone is Happy
Clear promises = less frustration
