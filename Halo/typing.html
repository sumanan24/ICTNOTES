📚 Learn Typing Basics

🎯 What is Touch Typing?

Touch typing is typing without looking at the keyboard. It involves using muscle memory to find keys, allowing you to type faster and more accurately while keeping your eyes on the screen.

⚡ Why Practice Typing?

Improving your typing speed and accuracy increases productivity, reduces errors, and makes computer work more efficient. Professional typists can reach 60-100+ words per minute!

💡 Key Principles

Focus on accuracy first, then speed. Use all ten fingers, maintain proper posture, and practice regularly. The home row is your foundation - learn it well!
